Easy objectives

These objectives grant 5 points each.

# Description Do it!
#1 Avoid running taps
#2 Reduce the time spent in the shower by 2 minutes
#3 Turn off the tap when soaping or washing your hairs
#4 Take a shower instead of a bath
#5 Turn off the tap when you brush your teeth
#6 Only start your washing machine when it is fully loaded
#7 Only start your dishwasher when it is fully loaded
#8 Only fill the kettle with the amount of water needed
#9 Reuse the water that has been left in the kettle
#10 If available, activate the Eco/Fast mode on your dishwasher
#11 If available, activate the Eco/Fast mode on you washing machine
#12 Use a glass of water to rinse your mouth when you clean your teeth
#13 If you have dual-flush toilets, make sure you use the long or short flush when it is appropriated
#14 When dishwashing per hand, try not to use too much soap or washing liquid

Medium objectives

These objectives grant 20 points each.

# Description Do it!
#1 Try not to take a bath this week
#2 Reduce the time spent in the shower by 3 minutes
#3 Try to do one less washing machine load this week
#4 Try to do one less dishwasher load this week
#5 Do not flush the toilet all the time
#6 Reuse the water used for boiling some food to water plant. Ex: eggs, pasta
#7 If dishwashing per hand, try to use a basin/bowl
#8 If dishwashing per hand, try to rinse the dishes in a bowl as well after washing them
#9 Try to reuse the water used for dishwashing per hand for garden purposes. A low amount of washing liquid won’t affect the plant
#10 Try to wash your clothes by hand in a basin when you don’t have a lot to clean
#11 Try to turn on the dishwasher or washing machine between hour#1 and hour#2

Difficult objectives

These objectives grant 50 points each.

# Description Do it!
#1 Collect rainwater to water your plants
#2 Try to do one less washing machine load per week over two weeks
#3 Try to do one less dishwasher load per week over two weeks
#4 Try only to take two baths maximum per month
#5 When taking the bath, try to reuse some of the “grey” water for garden purposes
#6 If you can, reduce the intensity of the flow coming out of the shower
#7 If washing your car, try to use a basin and not a pressure washer
